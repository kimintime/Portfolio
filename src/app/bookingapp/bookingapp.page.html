Booking App Laurea Digital Living Lab Booking App Project Microsoft PowerApps, Sharepoint Lists

App project for Laura's Digital Living Lab, allows users to checkout items from the lab, and for admins to maintain it. This was a group project, the project which already existed. Other group members saw to the lists and their contents, while My role was in rebuilding the application, and adding the ability to book, and for admins to handle bookings and returns.

As you have to have an account at Laurea to access the app, have a look at the app demo, and of course to browse its full documentation over at Github.
