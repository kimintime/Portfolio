About About Me

Hi, and thanks for clicking through to read more about me. I come from more creative pursuits, the world of music and theatre, and more recently that of developmental editing.

I will always carry these things with me, but the great thing about having that creative drive, is that it can find other outlets, such as software development. Like many of us in the tech world, I always had fun tinkering with computer stuff, but it was the AMKoodari program in 2020 where I discovered a true joy for it.

Deciding to make that transition from editing full time, to pursuing life as a software developer, I applied to Laurea’s BIT program, and have found success there. In IT though, if you want to learn more, you have to go out there and get it yourself, which lead me to Integrify Academy, where I've recently received certification in fullstack development. My tech stack there was React, Redux, Typescript, C# ASP.NET and Entity Framework, using PostgreSQL and Azure, and my goal is to continue to improve my skills, and learn new ones. Every level on my journey has been a challenge, but an exciting and welcome one.

Software Development doesn’t have an end goal, it’s rather in what you create, always learning new skills, honing old ones, and is a lifelong learning process, and that appeals to me. Life’s too short to not try new things, and to not enjoy what you do.

When I’m not working on expanding my developer knowledge and skills, I enjoy listening to and making music, mostly playing one of my ukuleles nowadays. I also enjoy crafting jewelry pieces, and photography.
